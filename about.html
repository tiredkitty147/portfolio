About Me

I am Tired Kitty (aka Hello Kitty, aka Kitty White!) and I was born on November 1, 1974 in London, England. I moved to the United States to pursue my american dream, which is to become a UX designer! Currently, I am a junior studying Computer Science and Economics at Brown University.

When I was in high school, I was interested in business and consulting and this led me to pursue internships in these industries. This semester, I've developed a passion for UX Design when I started taking a UI/UX course at Brown. This course guided me to pursue my career in UX design. My past experiences taught me to work collaboratively, come up with innovative solutions and help people to solve their challenges and I believe that I can transfer these skills to UX Design. I am always working to improve my design skills, and keep up with new trends and technologies.
